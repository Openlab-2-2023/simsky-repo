General Information

Darth Vader, formerly known as Anakin Skywalker, is a Sith Lord with decades of experience in galactic domination, leading Imperial forces, and wielding the Dark Side of the Force. Known for his exceptional leadership and strong negotiation skills—often utilizing a "persuasive" Force choke—he’s helped build the Galactic Empire into a (mostly) functioning tyranny.
